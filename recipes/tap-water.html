Tap Water

A Low calorie drink for the average man!

if the heat is too much for you or perhaps the cold is taking over you, then we have the perfect beverage for you! Depending on your taste and needs you can actually change the temperature of this beverage and it still stays all that refreshing, low calorie and you can make it with minimal preparation time! This is also something you can actually serve your kids and they will like it!

Ingredients

  • cup water

  • tablespoons water (additional if needed)

Directions

  1. Place glass under tap.

  2. Turn tap on.

  3. Fill glass with water almost to top.

  4. Turn tap off.

  5. Top up glass with additional teaspoons of water until full(if the glass isn't already full)

  6. Use paper towel to mop up any spillage.

  7. Enjoy!
